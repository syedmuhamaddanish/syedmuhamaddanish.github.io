Hello!

I am currently employed as an Assistant Professor at Algoma University. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher and lecturer at York University under the supervision of Prof. Sotirios Liaskos. I received my Ph.D. from the Département de génie logiciel et des TI at the University ETS Montreal, Quebec, in August 2022, with Prof. Kaiwen Zhang as my advisor and Prof. Hans-Arno Jacobsen as my co-advisor. Before coming to ETS Montreal, I worked with Prof. Marios Lestas as a research assistant under the Erasmus+ mobility project at Frederick University, Cyprus. My hometown is Rawalpindi, Pakistan.

I work on green energy systems, where my main goal is to provide green energy solutions to reduce overall carbon footprints.

My principal interests include developing strategies to expand the integration of renewable energy sources and electric vehicles into the existing energy grid, providing solutions for the efficient use of energy infrastructure, and integrating blockchain networks. Recently, I have also started working on reducing the energy consumption of generative AI models, such as large language models (LLMs).

Also, I recently received the NSERC Discovery Grant (DG) for a period of five years.
